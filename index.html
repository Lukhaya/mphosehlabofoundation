Prototype by A.L JENETE for #MphoSehlaboFoundation

It first started as an encounter, which turned into an idea, then a dream, and now a reality. I still remember so vividly the first day I took my pen and notebook, and drafted the plan to create this astounding initiative...see more

I only had one goal in mind- CHANGE A LIFE. Even if it’s just ONE! I grappled with this idea for months The idea of creating something that will serve as a beacon of hope to the marginalized and encourage them to believe that they can achieve anything in life despite their socio-economic status and native environments. The requisite to change the lives of young people- scholars & students, orphans and the homeless. Today, the #MphoSehlaboFoundation has partnered with the University of Cape Town to donate basic essentials to students experiencing funding crisis. With the emergent issues surrounding funding and NSFAS all over South Africa, we hope that this initiative will change the lives of UCT community and universities around the country. We are so honored to have been given the opportunity to make a difference in the student’s lives.

AIM

Mpho Sehlabo Foundation is an initiative that was created to eradicate poverty for scholars, students, the homeless and orphans from the most marginalized and vulnerable communities.
